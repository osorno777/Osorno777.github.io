This Advertisement is Intended only for Residents of California, Georgia, and South Carolina!!!

Term insurance offers no cash value or investment return but can be useful for temporary (especially 10 year to 20 year) protection needs. Nevertheless, the differences in rates can be astounding, even for essentially the same quality.  For instance, Dr. Cobin has replaced AAA Auto Club and Primerica policies with rates as much as double or even triple those of other companies of equal or better quality.  Five of Dr. Cobin's favorite AA-rated (high quality) carriers are First Colony Life Insurance Co. [AA] (Colony series products), Zurich (Kemper) Life [AA] (Certain-T 2001 series products), Security-Connecticut Life Insurance Co. [AA+], The Old Line Life Insurance Co. of America [AA+], and Lincoln Benefit Life Co. [AA+] (GT Gold series products).  Policies of $250,000 can run as little as $10 to $16 monthly (with premiums guaranteed not to rise for 10 years) for healthy, non-smokers under age 48.
